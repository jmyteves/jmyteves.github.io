# Joji Marie Teves, PhD - Personal Website

**Live Site:** [jmyteves.github.io](https://jmyteves.github.io)

Personal website showcasing my research in cell and molecular biology, career journey across four countries, and my projects. 
## 🧬 About

I'm a cell and molecular biologist currently working as a Visiting Postdoctoral Researcher at IDIBELL (Barcelona), studying endometrial regeneration and cellular identity. This website serves as my professional portfolio and research hub.

### Research Interests
- **Chromatin Biology** - How cells encode identity
- **Cellular Plasticity** - How cells reprogram and change identity  
- **Regenerative Biology** - How tissues rebuild after injury
